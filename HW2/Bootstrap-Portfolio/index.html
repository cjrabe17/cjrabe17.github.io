About Me

Cassandra prefers to go by Cassie--unless a telemarketer is calling or her mom is yelling at her. Her last name is pronounced like Robbie (rah-bee) and is always difficult to explain to people. She has a passion for reading (books, graphic novels, long Buzzfeed articles), spending her undergraduate and graduate career studying literature and English education. After college, she taught middle school language arts in Richmond, Virginia for several years before returning to her home state of Florida in 2016. She now lives in Winter Park with her husband, Nick. She ultimately decided to leave the classroom in order to pursue an education in web development. She hopes to one day marry her newfound technical skills with her background in education to enhance the technology used by students and teachers alike.
